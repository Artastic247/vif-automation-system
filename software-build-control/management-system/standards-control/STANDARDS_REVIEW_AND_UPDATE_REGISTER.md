# STANDARDS_REVIEW_AND_UPDATE_REGISTER

## Purpose
Record review triggers, ownership, changes, affected artefacts and required updates for standards/guidance lenses, customer-specific requirements and app-specific requirements.

## Scope
All standards-control entries and their impact on procedures, work instructions, validators, app-development controls, claims and management review.

## Owner/agent
Compliance Claim Approver with Organisational Knowledge Controller.

## Inputs
Standard/guidance item, review trigger, source update, customer requirement update, app requirement update, audit finding, RCA/CAPA, management-review action, affected artefacts and affected risks.

## Activities/method
1. Record standard/guidance item.
2. Identify review trigger.
3. Assign owner.
4. Record last review and next review.
5. Summarise change or mark no change.
6. Identify affected artefacts and risks.
7. Define required update.
8. Assign status and evidence.
9. Escalate claim or source-risk issues.

## Outputs/records
Standards review/update register, affected artefact list, risk update, knowledge update and management-review input.

## Linked processes
Standards control, organisational knowledge, audit, RCA/CAPA, management review, app onboarding and release.

## Linked skills/WIs
WI_021_INTERNAL_AUDIT, WI_023_RCA, WI_024_CAPA_EFFECTIVENESS_REVIEW, WI_025_CONTINUAL_IMPROVEMENT, WI_027_ORGANISATIONAL_KNOWLEDGE_UPDATE and WI_028_VALIDATOR_CALIBRATION.

## Linked gates
Standards review gate, knowledge update gate, claim-control gate, app onboarding gate and management-review gate.

## Evidence required
Review trigger, owner, review date, change summary, affected artefacts, required update, status and approval.

## Risks
Outdated interpretation, obsolete control, claim wording not updated, customer requirement missed, app-specific source ignored.

## Controls
Standards/source changes must be assessed for affected processes, WIs, validators, app onboarding, release and claim-control impact.

## Interfaces
Standards specialists, Knowledge Controller, QA Gatekeeper, App Owner, Customer Requirement Owner, App-Specific Compliance Owner, Management Review Owner.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when review/update is complete and evidence exists. HOLD while source/update/review remains pending. BLOCKED when outdated or unsupported requirement is used for claim, onboarding or release.

## Escalation
Escalate overdue high-risk review, claim impact, customer requirement ambiguity, app requirement ambiguity or unsupported wording.

## MLA / maturity dependency
M3+ requires scheduled review evidence. M4/M5 require trend and effectiveness evidence.

## Update trigger
Source update, standards/guidance change, customer/app requirement change, audit finding, RCA/CAPA, management-review action or claim request.
