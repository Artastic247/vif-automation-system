# WI_025_CONTINUAL_IMPROVEMENT

## Purpose
Convert findings, lessons, failures and opportunities into controlled improvement actions with owners, evidence and effectiveness review.

## Scope
Audit findings, RCA/CAPA outputs, lessons learned, prompt failures, AI bad outputs, validator gaps, release issues, customer feedback, support incidents and management-review actions.

## When to use
Use when a trend, failure, opportunity, customer feedback or management-review decision requires systemic improvement.

## When not to use
Do not use as feature creep or uncontrolled app change; improvements must be routed through change/job-card control.

## Owner/agent
Continual Improvement Owner with QA Gatekeeper and Management Review Owner.

## Inputs
Finding/source, risk/priority, affected process/module/tool/prompt/skill/agent/validator, expected result, evidence required, owner and due date.

## Method steps
1. Capture improvement source.
2. Classify risk/priority.
3. Define affected artefacts/processes.
4. Assign owner and due date.
5. Define expected result and evidence.
6. Route through job card/change control if implementation is needed.
7. Verify implementation.
8. Review effectiveness.
9. Update lessons and organisational knowledge.

## Outputs/records
Improvement action, implementation record, effectiveness record, knowledge update and management-review input.

## Evidence required
Improvement source, action record, implementation evidence, effectiveness review, closure approval.

## Linked ADP processes
ADP-20 and ADP-21 plus affected ADP process.

## Linked MOP/COP/SOP processes
Clause 10 continual improvement, Clause 9 monitoring/management review and Clause 7 knowledge.

## Linked gates
Improvement intake, change/job-card, effectiveness, management-review and maturity gates.

## Tools allowed
Improvement register, CI action tracker, lessons learned, RCA/CAPA, KPI register, management-review actions.

## Tools prohibited
Uncontrolled direct implementation, app-repo changes while onboarding HOLD, auto-fix/merge/release.

## Risks
Improvement becomes feature creep, action has no owner, no effectiveness evidence, repeated failures persist.

## Controls
Improvement must have source, owner, evidence, closure rule and effectiveness review.

## Interfaces
Continual Improvement Owner, RCA/CAPA Specialist, Knowledge Controller, Prompt/Skill/Validator Owners, Management Review Owner.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when improvement is implemented and effective. HOLD when action/evidence is open. BLOCKED when critical repeat failure has no improvement action.

## Escalation
Escalate overdue critical improvement, repeated failure, ineffective action or management-review action overdue.

## MLA / maturity dependency
M4/M5 maturity requires trend-based improvement and effectiveness evidence; lower maturity may remain HOLD.

## Update trigger
New finding, RCA/CAPA, lesson, KPI trend, customer feedback, audit finding or management review.
