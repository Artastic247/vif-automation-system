# WI_004_PROCESS_MAPPING

## Purpose
Map software-build and app-development processes using the process approach so work is controlled by purpose, inputs, activities, outputs, owners, evidence and gates.

## Scope
MOP, COP, SOP and ADP processes.

## When to use
Use when defining a new process, updating an existing process, preparing app onboarding, building turtles or auditing process adequacy.

## When not to use
Do not use as a generic diagram exercise without owners, evidence, measures, risks, controls and interfaces.

## Owner/agent
Process Engineer with Process Owner and QA Gatekeeper.

## Inputs
Process purpose, trigger, inputs, activities, outputs, owner, tools, methods, KPIs, risks, controls, records, interfaces, gates and PDCA loop.

## Method steps
1. Define process purpose and scope.
2. Identify trigger and input.
3. Define activities in controlled sequence.
4. Define output and customer/receiver.
5. Assign owner and supporting roles.
6. Identify resources/tools and methods/skills.
7. Define KPI or measure.
8. Define risk and control.
9. Define record/evidence.
10. Define interface and handoff.
11. Define gate and escalation.
12. Link PDCA.

## Outputs/records
Process map, turtle row, interface record, KPI/risk/control record and gate linkage.

## Evidence required
Completed process map/turtle, owner approval, evidence expectations and interface/gate records.

## Linked ADP processes
All ADP processes, especially ADP-01 to ADP-06 and ADP-21.

## Linked MOP/COP/SOP processes
Clause 4 process approach, Clause 5 ownership, Clause 6 planning, Clause 8 operation, Clause 9 audit and Clause 10 improvement.

## Linked gates
Process-definition gate, turtle gate, interface gate and audit gate.

## Tools allowed
Process map, turtle matrix, interface map, whiteboard/diagram tools, GitHub-controlled artefacts.

## Tools prohibited
Uncontrolled diagrams that are not linked to records, owners, risks or gates.

## Risks
Process shell, missing owner, no evidence, no KPI, no interface, no gate, document-only control.

## Controls
A process is not accepted unless input, activity, output, owner, evidence, KPI, risk/control, interface and gate are defined.

## Interfaces
Process owner, downstream process owner, QA Gatekeeper, Evidence Auditor and Management Review Owner.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when map is complete and approved. HOLD when supporting evidence is incomplete. BLOCKED when process is used operationally without owner/control/evidence.

## Escalation
Escalate missing owner, missing evidence, uncontrolled interface or repeated process failure.

## MLA / maturity dependency
M0 no process. M1 draft map. M2 pilot process. M3 released process. M4 managed with KPIs. M5 optimised with improvement evidence.

## Update trigger
New process, process change, audit finding, RCA/CAPA, KPI trend or management-review action.
