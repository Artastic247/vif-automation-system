# WI_026_LESSONS_LEARNED_UPDATE

## Purpose
Capture lessons learned and convert them into prevention rules, artefact updates and recurrence checks.

## Scope
Failures, audits, RCA/CAPA outputs, prompt drift, AI bad outputs, validator misses, release/rollback issues, customer feedback and support incidents.

## When to use
Use after RCA/CAPA, repeat failure, major audit finding, failed validation, failed release/rollback, support incident or significant improvement.

## When not to use
Do not use as a note-only record; every significant lesson must identify prevention action or justify no action.

## Owner/agent
Lessons Learned Controller with RCA/CAPA Specialist and Organisational Knowledge Controller.

## Inputs
Failure/event, cause, evidence, affected process, affected artefact, RCA/CAPA output, recurrence risk and improvement need.

## Method steps
1. Record failure/event and evidence.
2. Record cause or linked RCA.
3. Define prevention rule.
4. Identify artefact updated.
5. Identify process, prompt, skill/WI or validator update.
6. Assign owner and due date.
7. Define recurrence check.
8. Link effectiveness review.
9. Update organisational knowledge.

## Outputs/records
Lesson learned record, prevention rule, updated artefact reference, recurrence check and effectiveness review link.

## Evidence required
Event evidence, cause/RCA link, update evidence, recurrence check, effectiveness decision.

## Linked ADP processes
ADP-20 and ADP-21 plus affected ADP process.

## Linked MOP/COP/SOP processes
Clause 10 lessons learned, Clause 7 organisational knowledge and Clause 9 management review.

## Linked gates
Lessons learned gate, improvement gate, knowledge update gate and effectiveness gate.

## Tools allowed
Lessons learned register, RCA/CAPA records, improvement register, prompt/skill/process/validator registers.

## Tools prohibited
Leaving lessons as unowned notes, using lessons to justify uncontrolled scope, ignoring repeat failures.

## Risks
Lesson not embedded, recurrence, stale knowledge, repeated prompt/tool failure, validator gap remains.

## Controls
Significant lessons require owner, updated artefact, recurrence check and effectiveness review.

## Interfaces
RCA/CAPA Specialist, Knowledge Controller, Prompt Owner, Skill/WI Owner, Validator Owner, Process Owner.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when lesson is converted into effective control. HOLD when update/effectiveness evidence pending. BLOCKED when repeated critical failure lacks lesson/control update.

## Escalation
Escalate repeat failure, unimplemented high-risk lesson, ineffective prevention rule or missed knowledge update.

## MLA / maturity dependency
M3+ requires released lessons process. M4/M5 require trends showing lessons reduce recurrence.

## Update trigger
RCA/CAPA closure, repeat issue, audit finding, support incident, UAT/release failure or management-review action.
