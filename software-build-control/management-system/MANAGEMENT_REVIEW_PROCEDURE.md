# MANAGEMENT_REVIEW_PROCEDURE.md

## Purpose
Define the management-review process for evaluating the suitability, adequacy, effectiveness, maturity and improvement needs of the Software Build Control System.

## Scope
Applies to management-system performance, process effectiveness, audit results, RCA/CAPA effectiveness, AI governance, prompt governance, validator effectiveness, release readiness, automation readiness and strategic improvement decisions.

## Owner/agent
System owner chairs management review. QA Gatekeeper provides audit/performance inputs. AI Governance Owner provides AI governance inputs. Process owners provide KPI and risk inputs.

## Inputs
Performance evaluation outputs, audit programme/results, audit findings, KPI trends, maturity assessments, validation reports, RCA/CAPA status, AI bad-output monitoring, prompt failures, release/rollback records, lessons learned and strategic risks/opportunities.

## Activities/checklist
1. Review previous actions and effectiveness.
2. Review process performance and KPI trends.
3. Review audit results and repeated findings.
4. Review RCA/CAPA effectiveness.
5. Review maturity levels and automation requests.
6. Review AI governance, prompt governance and validator effectiveness.
7. Review risks, opportunities and contingency status.
8. Review resource, competence, knowledge and tooling needs.
9. Decide actions, priorities, escalation or maturity changes.

## Outputs/records
Management-review minutes, action register, escalation decisions, maturity decisions, improvement priorities and resource decisions.

## Maturity level
M3+ processes require periodic management review. M4/M5 require trend and optimisation review.

## Evidence required
Performance records, audit records, RCA/CAPA evidence, KPI trends, maturity evidence, AI/prompt reports and management-review action tracking.

## Linked process
MOP-06 Management review, MOP-05 Internal audit, MOP-07 Corrective action and continual improvement, MOP-03 Planning/risk/opportunity.

## Linked agent/skill/procedure/module
System owner, QA Gatekeeper, AI Governance Owner, Process Engineer, Lessons Learned Controller, management-review skill and maturity assessment.

## Interface/control point
Interfaces with performance evaluation, audit programme, RCA/CAPA, maturity model, AI governance, validator governance and continual improvement.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
- PASS: review inputs are complete, actions assigned and strategic decisions documented.
- HOLD: review incomplete or actions not fully assigned.
- BLOCKED: critical findings, repeated failures, uncontrolled AI risk, weak evidence or unsafe automation route unresolved.

## PDCA
- PLAN: define review agenda and inputs.
- DO: conduct management review.
- CHECK: evaluate effectiveness, maturity and strategic risk.
- ACT: assign actions, update priorities, revise controls and trigger improvement.

## Audit frequency
Periodic and risk-triggered per audit programme and management needs.

## Automation allowance
Management review may approve or block automation readiness based on maturity, audit evidence, RCA/CAPA effectiveness and strategic risk.

## Escalation
Escalate unresolved critical findings, repeated failures, ineffective CAPA, maturity overstatement, automation risk, release instability or uncontrolled AI behaviour.

## Update trigger
Audit result, RCA/CAPA trend, maturity change, validator issue, AI/prompt issue, release issue, strategic risk or lesson learned.
