# IMPROVEMENT_REGISTER.md

## Purpose
Provide a controlled register for continual-improvement actions and operational-learning actions.

## Scope
Applies to improvements raised from audits, RCA/CAPA, management review, AI governance, prompt governance, validator governance, release issues, maturity assessments and lessons learned.

## Owner/agent
Lessons Learned Controller.

## Inputs
Audit findings, CAPA records, KPI trends, management-review actions, AI/prompt failures, validation reports, maturity reviews and lessons learned.

## Outputs/records
Improvement action records, implementation evidence, effectiveness evidence and closure status.

## Maturity level
M4/M5 require active improvement evidence.

## Evidence required
Action evidence, effectiveness evidence and linked records.

## Linked process
MOP-07 Corrective action and continual improvement.

## Linked agent/skill/procedure/module
Lessons Learned Controller, Process Engineer and continual-improvement procedure.

## Interface/control point
Interfaces with RCA/CAPA, audits, management review, maturity assessment and lessons learned.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
- PASS: improvement effective and closed.
- HOLD: action in progress.
- BLOCKED: ineffective or overdue critical improvement.

## PDCA
- PLAN: identify improvement.
- DO: implement.
- CHECK: verify effectiveness.
- ACT: standardise and improve further.

## Audit frequency
Reviewed during audits and management review.

## Automation allowance
Automation blocked where critical improvements remain open.

## Escalation
Escalate overdue or ineffective improvements.

## Update trigger
Audit result, CAPA, KPI trend, AI/prompt issue or management-review action.
