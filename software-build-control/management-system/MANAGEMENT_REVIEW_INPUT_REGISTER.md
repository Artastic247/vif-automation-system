# MANAGEMENT_REVIEW_INPUT_REGISTER.md

## Purpose
Provide a controlled register for management-review inputs and decisions.

## Scope
Applies to process performance, audit findings, RCA/CAPA, maturity review, AI governance, prompt governance, validator effectiveness, release readiness and automation readiness.

## Owner/agent
System owner and QA Gatekeeper.

## Inputs
Performance evaluation outputs, audit outputs, KPI trends, RCA/CAPA records, AI bad-output records, prompt failure records and maturity assessments.

## Outputs/records
Management-review actions, escalations, improvement priorities and maturity decisions.

## Maturity level
M3+ mandatory.

## Evidence required
Linked records, audit references, KPI evidence and action tracking.

## Linked process
MOP-06 Management review.

## Linked agent/skill/procedure/module
System owner, QA Gatekeeper and management-review skill.

## Interface/control point
Interfaces with audit findings, RCA/CAPA, maturity assessment and continual improvement.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
- PASS: inputs reviewed and actions assigned.
- HOLD: incomplete input review.
- BLOCKED: unresolved critical issue or unsafe automation/release risk.

## PDCA
- PLAN: collect review inputs.
- DO: review and assign actions.
- CHECK: verify completion/effectiveness.
- ACT: escalate and improve.

## Audit frequency
Per management-review cycle.

## Automation allowance
No automation approval without management-review evidence.

## Escalation
Escalate unresolved critical issues and repeated failures.

## Update trigger
New audit, KPI trend, RCA/CAPA, AI/prompt issue or strategic change.
