# MATURITY_IMPROVEMENT_DOWNGRADE_FLOW

## Purpose
Define maturity upgrade and downgrade logic linked to improvement effectiveness.

## Scope
Processes, validators, prompts, procedures, workflows, onboarding readiness, CI readiness and automation permission.

## Owner/agent
MLA Assessor.

## Inputs
MLA assessment register, audit findings, RCA/CAPA, effectiveness reviews and continual-improvement actions.

## Activities/method
Define maturity upgrade trigger, downgrade trigger, required evidence, required audit, management approval required, automation-permission impact, rollback of automation permission, linkage to MLA register, RCA/CAPA, audit findings and reassessment cadence.

## Outputs/records
Approved maturity update or downgrade decision.

## Evidence required
Audit evidence, effectiveness evidence and approval records.

## Linked process
Clause 9 MLA and Clause 10 improvement.

## Linked gate
Maturity-governance gate.

## MLA / maturity dependency
This file governs maturity movement and automation permission.

## Clause 9 input
MLA assessments and audit results.

## Clause 10 output
Controlled maturity improvement or downgrade.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when maturity evidence supports decision. HOLD when reassessment pending. BLOCKED when overstated maturity remains unresolved.

## Escalation
Escalate repeated ineffective CAPA or unsupported maturity claims.

## Management-review input/output
Critical maturity changes feed management review.

## Organisational-knowledge impact
Maturity lessons update organisational knowledge.

## Update trigger
Audit result, RCA/CAPA closure, repeat failure or management decision.
