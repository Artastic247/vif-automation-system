# ORGANISATIONAL_KNOWLEDGE_UPDATE_FLOW

## Purpose
Define how validated learning is converted into controlled organisational knowledge.

## Scope
Knowledge affecting prompts, skills/WIs, procedures, agents, validators, standards-control, app onboarding, CI readiness, n8n readiness, release/rollback and security/data controls.

## Owner/agent
Organisational Knowledge Controller.

## Inputs
RCA/CAPA outcomes, lessons learned, effectiveness reviews, management-review actions, audit findings and tool/provider changes.

## Activities/method
Classify knowledge item, identify affected artefacts, update controlled documents, assign owner, require review/approval, communicate change to relevant agents/tools, verify adoption and archive superseded guidance.

## Outputs/records
Knowledge update record, updated artefacts, revision note, communication evidence and adoption verification.

## Evidence required
Source record, update evidence, approval record, communication/adoption evidence and superseded artefact control.

## Linked process
Clause 7 organisational knowledge and Clause 10 improvement.

## Linked gate
Knowledge update gate.

## MLA / maturity dependency
Managed/optimised maturity requires knowledge to be maintained and audited.

## Clause 9 input
Audit findings, management-review inputs and maturity gaps.

## Clause 10 output
Controlled knowledge update and prevention of repeat error.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when knowledge update is approved and adopted. HOLD when update/review is pending. BLOCKED when obsolete guidance could enable unsafe work.

## Escalation
Escalate obsolete critical guidance, conflicting instructions or repeated failure caused by stale knowledge.

## Management-review input
Knowledge gaps and adoption failures feed management review.

## Continual-improvement input
Knowledge update needs become improvement actions when systemic.

## Update trigger
RCA/CAPA closure, lesson learned, standard/tool change, audit finding or process change.
