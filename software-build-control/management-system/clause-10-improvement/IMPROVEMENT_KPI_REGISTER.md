# IMPROVEMENT_KPI_REGISTER

## Purpose
Define and track improvement KPIs that show whether the management system is becoming more effective.

## Scope
KPIs for NC recurrence, false PASS, prompt drift, validator misses, release blocks, rollback failures, audit findings, CAPA effectiveness, app onboarding readiness, CI/n8n readiness and repeat software-build failure patterns.

## Owner/agent
Continual Improvement Owner with Management Review Owner.

## Inputs
NC register, RCA/CAPA register, effectiveness review register, audit findings, validator reports, repeat-failure register and management-review actions.

## Activities/method
Maintain fields: KPI ID, KPI name, definition, source data, target, current result, trend, owner, review frequency, action trigger, linked improvement action, management-review status.

## Outputs/records
KPI register, trend review and action triggers.

## Evidence required
Source data, calculation basis, trend evidence, decision/action records.

## Linked process
Clause 10 continual improvement and Clause 9 monitoring.

## Linked gate
Improvement KPI gate.

## MLA / maturity dependency
M4/M5 maturity requires KPIs, trend review and effective action from trends.

## Clause 9 input
Monitoring/measurement results and audit findings.

## Clause 10 output
Improvement prioritisation and effectiveness evidence.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when KPI is measured, reviewed and acted on. HOLD when data/action is incomplete. BLOCKED when critical trend worsens without action.

## Escalation
Escalate worsening critical trends or missing KPI evidence for managed maturity claims.

## Management-review input
KPI trends and missed targets feed management review.

## Continual-improvement input
KPI failures trigger improvement actions.

## Update trigger
Measurement cycle, audit finding, management review or maturity claim.
