# CI_ACTION_TRACKER

## Purpose
Track continual-improvement actions from intake to verified closure.

## Scope
All Clause 10 continual-improvement actions.

## Owner/agent
Continual Improvement Owner.

## Inputs
Improvement source, risk/priority, owner, due date and affected artefacts.

## Activities/method
Maintain action ID, source, risk/priority, owner, due date, affected module/process, affected prompt, affected skill/WI, affected agent, affected validator/check, affected tool/provider, expected result, evidence required, status, effectiveness review, management-review link and closure approval.

## Outputs/records
Tracked improvement actions and closure evidence.

## Evidence required
Implementation evidence, effectiveness review and approval evidence.

## Linked process
Clause 10 continual improvement.

## Linked gate
Improvement-action gate.

## MLA / maturity dependency
M4/M5 maturity requires tracked and effective improvement actions.

## Clause 9 input
Audit findings, MLA gaps and KPI trends.

## Clause 10 output
Controlled improvement implementation and closure.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when action closed with evidence. HOLD while open. BLOCKED when critical action has no owner or evidence.

## Escalation
Escalate overdue or ineffective actions.

## Management-review input/output
Open critical actions feed management review.

## Organisational-knowledge impact
Closed actions may require knowledge updates.

## Update trigger
Action creation, update, review or closure.
