# MANAGEMENT_REVIEW_OUTPUT_ACTIONS

## Purpose
Control management-review outputs so decisions become assigned, evidenced and closed actions.

## Scope
Actions from management review relating to risks, resources, MLA, audits, app onboarding readiness, validator calibration, CI/n8n readiness, CAPA effectiveness, tool/provider performance, security/data protection and improvement priorities.

## Owner/agent
Management Review Owner with assigned action owners.

## Inputs
Management-review input register, KPI trends, audit findings, MLA status, NC/CAPA status and improvement proposals.

## Activities/method
Record decision, define action, assign owner, set due date, define evidence required, link affected process/artefact, track completion, verify effectiveness and close or escalate.

## Outputs/records
Management-review action record, implementation evidence, effectiveness result and escalation if overdue.

## Evidence required
Review decision, action evidence, owner confirmation, effectiveness evidence and closure approval.

## Linked process
Clause 9 management review and Clause 10 improvement.

## Linked gate
Management-review action closure gate.

## MLA / maturity dependency
Open critical management actions may block maturity upgrade and automation permission.

## Clause 9 input
Management-review inputs, audit and KPI trends.

## Clause 10 output
Assigned improvement/resource/risk actions and closure evidence.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when action is closed with evidence. HOLD while open. BLOCKED when critical overdue action affects safety, release, CI/n8n or customer-data control.

## Escalation
Escalate overdue critical action, ignored systemic risk or repeated ineffective action.

## Management-review input
Open actions and overdue actions return as next review inputs.

## Continual-improvement input
Strategic review decisions feed continual-improvement register.

## Update trigger
Management review, action update, due-date breach or effectiveness decision.
