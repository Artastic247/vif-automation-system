# CLAUSE_10_IMPROVEMENT_INTERFACE_MATRIX

## Purpose
Define interfaces across Clause 9 and Clause 10 governance flows.

## Scope
Interfaces between audit findings, MLA, NC, correction, RCA, CAPA, effectiveness review, repeat-failure review, continual improvement, lessons learned, organisational knowledge, process/prompt/skill/agent/validator updates, management review, app onboarding readiness, GitHub CI readiness and n8n readiness.

## Owner/agent
RCA/CAPA Specialist.

## Inputs
Clause 9 outputs, NC records, RCA/CAPA records and management-review actions.

## Activities/method
For each interface define input, output, sender, receiver, control point, evidence required, risk, maturity dependency and gate decision.

## Outputs/records
Controlled interface traceability matrix.

## Evidence required
Linked records, approvals, update evidence and gate decisions.

## Linked process
Clause 9 and Clause 10 integration.

## Linked gate
Improvement-interface gate.

## MLA / maturity dependency
Interface failures may block maturity progression and automation readiness.

## Clause 9 input
Audit findings, MLA assessments and validation evidence.

## Clause 10 output
Traceable improvement and knowledge-update flow.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when interfaces are traceable and evidenced. HOLD when linkage incomplete. BLOCKED when critical process bypass occurs.

## Escalation
Escalate broken interface control or unsupported gate bypass.

## Management-review input/output
Systemic interface failures feed management review.

## Organisational-knowledge impact
Interface lessons drive knowledge and validator updates.

## Update trigger
Audit update, RCA/CAPA change, onboarding rule change or governance revision.
