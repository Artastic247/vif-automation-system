# RCA_REGISTER

## Purpose
Track RCA records and root-cause decisions.

## Scope
All RCA activities linked to NCs, audit findings, validation failures, app defects and systemic failures.

## Owner/agent
RCA/CAPA Specialist.

## Inputs
Linked NC, evidence package, containment status and root-cause method.

## Activities/method
Maintain fields: RCA ID, linked NC, root-cause method, direct cause, systemic cause, escaped-detection cause, maturity-level cause, interface/control-point failure, contributing factors, evidence reviewed, corrective action required, owner, due date, status.

## Outputs/records
RCA register and action requirements.

## Evidence required
Evidence list, cause analysis, owner decision and action link.

## Linked process
Clause 10 RCA.

## Linked gate
RCA record gate.

## MLA / maturity dependency
Open systemic RCA may block M3+ release and automation permission.

## Clause 9 input
Findings and maturity concerns requiring cause analysis.

## Clause 10 output
Corrective-action requirement and effectiveness-review input.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when RCA is complete and action defined. HOLD while RCA open. BLOCKED when high-risk RCA lacks owner or due date.

## Escalation
Escalate overdue, repeat or high-risk RCA.

## Management-review input
RCA status and systemic themes feed management review.

## Continual-improvement input
Root-cause themes feed improvement planning.

## Update trigger
RCA opened, updated, closed or found ineffective.
