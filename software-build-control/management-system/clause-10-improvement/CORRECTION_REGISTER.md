# CORRECTION_REGISTER

## Purpose
Record immediate corrections and containment actions taken for nonconformities.

## Scope
Corrections for audit findings, validation failures, release blocks, security/data risks, prompt/tool issues and app defects.

## Owner/agent
Correction owner with QA Gatekeeper.

## Inputs
Linked NC, containment requirement, evidence, residual risk and verification need.

## Activities/method
Maintain fields: correction ID, linked NC, immediate correction taken, containment action, evidence, owner, date completed, verification of correction, residual risk, RCA/CAPA still required yes/no.

## Outputs/records
Correction record, containment evidence and residual-risk decision.

## Evidence required
Before/after evidence, containment proof, verification result and residual-risk assessment.

## Linked process
Clause 10 correction and containment.

## Linked gate
Correction verification gate.

## MLA / maturity dependency
Repeated corrections without RCA/CAPA can downgrade maturity.

## Clause 9 input
Audit or verification finding requiring immediate correction.

## Clause 10 output
Verified correction and RCA/CAPA decision.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when correction verified. HOLD when verification pending. BLOCKED when containment is missing for critical risk.

## Escalation
Escalate if correction fails, risk remains high or repeated correction indicates systemic issue.

## Management-review input
Frequent corrections and residual risks feed management review.

## Continual-improvement input
Correction patterns feed RCA and improvement backlog.

## Update trigger
NC creation, correction completion, verification result or recurrence.
