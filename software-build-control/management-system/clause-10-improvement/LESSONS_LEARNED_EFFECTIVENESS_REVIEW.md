# LESSONS_LEARNED_EFFECTIVENESS_REVIEW

## Purpose
Ensure lessons learned are not only recorded but converted into effective controls that prevent recurrence.

## Scope
Lessons from software-build failures, app defects, prompt failures, AI-output issues, validator failures, CI pilot findings, release/rollback issues, security/data risks and customer-change lessons.

## Owner/agent
Lessons Learned Controller with RCA/CAPA Specialist and Organisational Knowledge Controller.

## Inputs
Lessons learned register, RCA/CAPA output, repeat-failure register, effectiveness reviews, audit findings and management-review decisions.

## Activities/method
Identify lesson source, define prevention rule, assign affected artefacts, update prompt/skill/procedure/validator/process, verify use in later work, check recurrence, and classify lesson as effective, partially effective or ineffective.

## Outputs/records
Lesson effectiveness decision, updated controls, knowledge update and improvement action where needed.

## Evidence required
Lesson record, updated artefact, evidence of application, recurrence check and effectiveness decision.

## Linked process
Clause 10 lessons learned and continual improvement.

## Linked gate
Lessons learned effectiveness gate.

## MLA / maturity dependency
M4/M5 maturity requires proof that lessons drive updates and reduce recurrence.

## Clause 9 input
Audit findings, maturity downgrades and evidence-audit trends.

## Clause 10 output
Updated knowledge, controls and improvement actions.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when lesson is applied and recurrence control is evidenced. HOLD when update/use evidence is incomplete. BLOCKED when repeat failure occurs and lesson was not converted into control.

## Escalation
Escalate repeated failure despite lesson learned or unimplemented high-risk lesson.

## Management-review input
Lesson effectiveness and repeat-failure themes feed management review.

## Continual-improvement input
Ineffective lessons become improvement actions.

## Update trigger
New lesson, RCA/CAPA closure, recurrence, audit finding or management-review action.
