# EFFECTIVENESS_REVIEW_REGISTER

## Purpose
Track effectiveness reviews and recurrence outcomes.

## Scope
All CAPA actions requiring effectiveness verification.

## Owner/agent
RCA/CAPA Specialist.

## Inputs
Linked CAPA, operational evidence, KPI data and recurrence check.

## Activities/method
Maintain fields: review ID, linked CAPA, review date, evidence reviewed, recurrence found yes/no, KPI result, decision, required escalation, required maturity update, required lesson learned, closure status.

## Outputs/records
Effectiveness-review register and closure status.

## Evidence required
Operational evidence, KPI result, recurrence analysis and closure decision.

## Linked process
Clause 10 effectiveness review.

## Linked gate
Effectiveness-review closure gate.

## MLA / maturity dependency
Repeated ineffective reviews can downgrade maturity and block automation.

## Clause 9 input
Performance trends and audit follow-up.

## Clause 10 output
Verified effectiveness result and escalation decision.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when effectiveness proven. HOLD when monitoring remains open. BLOCKED when recurrence exists without escalation.

## Escalation
Escalate repeated ineffective actions or unresolved recurrence.

## Management-review input
Effectiveness trend and recurrence status feed management review.

## Continual-improvement input
Review outcomes feed lessons learned and organisational knowledge.

## Update trigger
Review scheduled, KPI update, recurrence or closure decision.
