# CONTINUAL_IMPROVEMENT_REGISTER

## Purpose
Track improvement actions from intake through verification and closure.

## Scope
All improvement opportunities and systemic actions affecting the control system, software-build process, validators, prompts, skills/WIs, agents, tools and app onboarding readiness.

## Owner/agent
Continual Improvement Owner.

## Inputs
Improvement source, risk/priority, affected process/module, expected result and required evidence.

## Activities/method
Maintain fields: improvement ID, source, risk/priority, owner, due date, affected module/process, expected result, evidence required, implementation control, effectiveness review, management-review link, closure rule and status.

## Outputs/records
Improvement register, action tracking, evidence trail and closure decision.

## Evidence required
Source evidence, implementation evidence, verification/effectiveness evidence and management-review link where applicable.

## Linked process
Clause 10 continual improvement.

## Linked gate
Improvement register gate.

## MLA / maturity dependency
Active improvement records and effectiveness evidence support M4/M5 maturity.

## Clause 9 input
Audit, monitoring, MLA and management-review findings.

## Clause 10 output
Prioritised improvement actions and effectiveness records.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when action is closed with evidence. HOLD while action is open. BLOCKED when high-risk systemic issue has no owner or due date.

## Escalation
Escalate overdue critical action, ineffective improvement or repeated recurrence.

## Management-review input
Open/overdue/closed improvement trends feed management review.

## Continual-improvement input
This register is the controlled improvement backlog and action tracker.

## Update trigger
New improvement, action update, effectiveness review or closure.
