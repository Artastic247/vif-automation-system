# EFFECTIVENESS_REVIEW_PROCEDURE

## Purpose
Verify whether corrective and preventive actions actually prevent recurrence and improve system performance.

## Scope
CAPA actions, validator updates, prompt/procedure/skill changes, release controls, onboarding controls and security/data actions.

## Owner/agent
RCA/CAPA Specialist with Management Review Owner.

## Inputs
Closed CAPA, operational evidence, KPI trends, recurrence evidence and updated maturity status.

## Activities/method
Review action reviewed, expected prevention result, evidence after use, recurrence check, KPI/metric, maturity-level impact, validation-rule impact, prompt/skill/agent/procedure impact, decision effective/partially effective/ineffective, escalation if ineffective, management-review input, lessons-learned update and organisational-knowledge update.

## Outputs/records
Effectiveness-review decision, escalation trigger and improvement actions.

## Evidence required
Operational evidence, recurrence check, KPI evidence, validation/audit evidence and closure approval.

## Linked process
Clause 10 effectiveness review.

## Linked gate
Effectiveness gate.

## MLA / maturity dependency
Effective actions may support maturity progression. Ineffective actions can force downgrade.

## Clause 9 input
Audit findings, KPI trends and MLA status.

## Clause 10 output
Effectiveness decision, escalation, lessons learned and knowledge update.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when recurrence is prevented with evidence. HOLD when monitoring is incomplete. BLOCKED when ineffective actions leave critical risk uncontrolled.

## Escalation
Escalate ineffective CAPA, repeated recurrence or failed KPI.

## Management-review input
Effectiveness trends feed management review.

## Continual-improvement input
Effective and ineffective actions drive continual-improvement priorities.

## Update trigger
CAPA closure, recurrence, KPI failure or audit finding.
