# CONTINUAL_IMPROVEMENT_PROCEDURE

## Purpose
Define how improvement opportunities are captured, prioritised, controlled, implemented and verified across the Software Build Management System.

## Scope
Applies to improvements from audits, MLA assessments, NC/RCA/CAPA, validator calibration, prompt drift, AI-output failures, release/rollback findings, app onboarding findings, support issues, tool/provider issues, lessons learned and management review.

## Owner/agent
Continual Improvement Owner with Management Review Owner, RCA/CAPA Specialist, QA Gatekeeper and Organisational Knowledge Controller.

## Inputs
Audit findings, NC/RCA/CAPA outputs, effectiveness reviews, repeat-failure trends, KPI results, lessons learned, management-review decisions and stakeholder feedback.

## Activities/method
Capture improvement source, classify risk/priority, assign owner, define expected result, link affected process/artefact, define evidence required, control implementation through job card/change control, verify effect, update knowledge, and close only when evidence proves improvement.

## Outputs/records
Improvement action, updated artefacts, verification evidence, management-review action closure and organisational-knowledge update.

## Evidence required
Improvement record, owner approval, implementation evidence, verification/effectiveness evidence and updated artefact links.

## Linked process
Clause 10 continual improvement.

## Linked gate
Improvement control gate.

## MLA / maturity dependency
M4/M5 maturity requires active trend-based improvement and evidence of effective actions.

## Clause 9 input
Audit trends, MLA trends, monitoring/measurement results, management-review inputs and validator/audit findings.

## Clause 10 output
Controlled improvement action, updated process/skill/prompt/validator/procedure and effectiveness evidence.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when improvement is implemented and effectiveness evidence exists. HOLD when action/evidence remains open. BLOCKED when repeated critical issue has no improvement route.

## Escalation
Escalate overdue critical improvements, repeat failures, ineffective CAPA or blocked management-review actions.

## Management-review input
Improvement status, overdue actions, effectiveness trends and systemic risks feed management review.

## Continual-improvement input
This procedure is the operating route for continual-improvement inputs.

## Update trigger
Audit trend, repeat failure, RCA/CAPA output, management review, validator calibration result or tool/process change.
