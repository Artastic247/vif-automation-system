# LESSONS_LEARNED_INTEGRATION_PROCEDURE.md

## Purpose
Define how lessons learned are captured, verified, classified, reused and integrated into processes, prompts, skills, validators, templates, AI controls and operational governance.

## Scope
Applies to all lessons learned from audits, RCA/CAPA, AI bad outputs, prompt failures, validator findings, release failures, onboarding failures, customer feedback, support issues and improvement actions.

## Owner/agent
Lessons Learned Controller owns the process. Process Engineer updates affected governance artefacts. QA Gatekeeper verifies integration effectiveness.

## Inputs
Audit findings, CAPA records, effectiveness reviews, AI bad-output records, prompt failure records, validator findings, release incidents, management-review actions and improvement records.

## Activities/checklist
1. Capture the lesson learned.
2. Classify source and impact.
3. Determine affected processes, prompts, skills, validators, templates, agents or controls.
4. Update the required artefacts.
5. Link the lesson to RCA/CAPA and maturity assessment where applicable.
6. Verify that the update reduced recurrence risk.
7. Escalate systemic lessons to management review.

## Outputs/records
Lesson learned record, updated artefact, linked CAPA/improvement action and effectiveness evidence.

## Maturity level
M4/M5 require active operational reuse of lessons learned.

## Evidence required
Lesson source, affected artefacts, update evidence, effectiveness evidence and recurrence review.

## Linked process
SOP-12 Lessons learned and knowledge reuse, MOP-07 Corrective action and continual improvement, MOP-06 Management review.

## Linked agent/skill/procedure/module
Lessons Learned Controller, Process Engineer, QA Gatekeeper, lessons-learned skill and continual-improvement procedure.

## Interface/control point
Interfaces with audits, RCA/CAPA, prompts, validators, AI governance, maturity assessment and process knowledge.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
- PASS: lesson integrated and recurrence risk reduced.
- HOLD: integration pending.
- BLOCKED: critical lesson ignored or repeated failure continues.

## PDCA
- PLAN: identify lesson.
- DO: integrate lesson.
- CHECK: verify effectiveness.
- ACT: standardise and escalate systemic issues.

## Audit frequency
Reviewed during audits, effectiveness review and management review.

## Automation allowance
Automation blocked where critical lessons remain unintegrated.

## Escalation
Escalate repeated failures, ignored lessons and systemic recurrence.

## Update trigger
Audit finding, CAPA, failed release, AI/prompt issue, validator issue or management-review decision.
