# AI_MODEL_TOOL_AUDIT_CHECKLIST

## Purpose
Audit approved AI models/tools and their suitability, boundaries, and governance controls.

## Scope
All AI models, coding tools, copilots, orchestration tools, LLM providers and AI-assisted workflows.

## Owner/agent
AI Model/Tool Auditor with ISO/IEC 42001 AI Management Specialist.

## Inputs
AI tool inventory, provider agreements, allowed/prohibited use rules, maturity level, prompt/output records and validation reports.

## Activities/method
Audit approved AI tool/model inventory, allowed/prohibited use, permitted data classification, human oversight, provider/tool change status, provider risk, prompt/output traceability, hallucination/false PASS control, customer-data restriction, evidence rules, maturity level, automation permission and ongoing suitability.

## Outputs/records
AI tool audit report, suitability decision, findings and RCA/CAPA trigger where required.

## Audit criteria
AI tools must operate within approved boundaries. SOURCE REQUIRED where official provider/security evidence is unavailable.

## Evidence required
Tool inventory, provider evidence, approval records, prompt/output examples, risk review and oversight evidence.

## MLA / maturity dependency
Automation use requires maturity approval. Experimental tools remain limited to pilot controls.

## Linked process
AI governance and Clause 9 AI/tool audit.

## Linked gate
AI tool approval gate.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when approved use and oversight evidence exist. HOLD when evidence is incomplete. BLOCKED when customer data, uncontrolled automation or unsupported claims are involved.

## Escalation
Escalate hallucination risk, provider breach, unsupported AI claim or unsafe automation.

## Management-review input
AI tool risks, provider changes and repeated output failures feed management review.

## Update trigger
Provider/tool change, incident, audit finding, maturity change or onboarding request.
