# MLA_AUDIT_REQUIREMENTS

## Purpose
Link maturity level, risk, and audit frequency.

## Scope
All maturity-assessed items including processes, agents, skills, validators, app onboarding, CI, n8n and release readiness.

## Owner/agent
MLA Assessor and Internal Audit Specialist.

## Inputs
MLA record, risk level, prior findings, automation exposure, customer/data impact.

## Activities/method
M0/M1 require gap review. M2 requires pilot audit. M3 requires scheduled audit. M4 requires KPI/effectiveness audit. M5 requires risk-based audit and automation review.

## Audit criteria
Audit frequency must match maturity, risk, and exposure.

## Evidence required
Maturity record, risk record, audit programme entry, finding history.

## MLA / maturity dependency
MLA drives audit frequency and scope.

## Linked process
Clause 9 audit programme.

## Linked gate
Audit planning gate.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when audit need is defined. HOLD when frequency or scope is missing. BLOCKED when high-risk automation/app onboarding lacks audit coverage.

## Escalation
Overdue audit, missing audit coverage, or high-risk M3+ item without audit.

## Management-review input
Audit coverage gaps and overdue audits feed management review.

## Update trigger
Maturity change, risk change, incident, automation request.
