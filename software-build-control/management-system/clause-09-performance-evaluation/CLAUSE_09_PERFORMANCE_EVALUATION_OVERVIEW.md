# CLAUSE_09_PERFORMANCE_EVALUATION_OVERVIEW

## Purpose
Define monitoring, measurement, audit, MLA, management-review inputs and performance evaluation for the Software Build Management System.

## Scope
Processes, apps, agents, skills, prompts, context packs, validators, tools, AI outputs, information security, release, CI and n8n readiness.

## Owner/agent
Internal Audit Specialist and Management Review Owner.

## Inputs
KPIs, validation reports, audits, incidents, RCA/CAPA, MLA records, release records, app onboarding records.

## Activities/method
Monitor, measure, analyse, evaluate, audit, assess maturity, classify findings, feed management review and continual improvement.

## Audit criteria
Use approved procedures, job cards, evidence registers, risk controls and standards lenses only. Do not claim certification or compliance.

## Evidence required
Current records, objective evidence, audit outputs, validation reports, maturity evidence.

## MLA / maturity dependency
Audit depth, frequency and automation permission depend on maturity level and risk.

## Linked process
Clause 9 performance evaluation.

## Linked gate
Performance evaluation gate.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS if evidence is current and reviewed. HOLD if evidence is partial. BLOCKED if evidence gaps permit unsafe app onboarding, release, app-repo CI or n8n.

## Escalation
Critical audit finding, false PASS, maturity overstatement, security/data risk.

## Management-review input
All major trends, findings, maturity changes and blocked gates feed management review.

## Update trigger
Audit programme, release, incident, validation change, management review.
