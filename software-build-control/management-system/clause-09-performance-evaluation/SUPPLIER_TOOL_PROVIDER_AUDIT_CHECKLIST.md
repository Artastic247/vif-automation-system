# SUPPLIER_TOOL_PROVIDER_AUDIT_CHECKLIST

## Purpose
Audit external suppliers, tools and providers used in the software-build system.

## Scope
GitHub, AI providers, deployment providers, orchestration tools, validation tools and related external services.

## Owner/agent
Supplier/Tool Provider Auditor.

## Inputs
Approved provider list, contracts, usage rules, outage history, cost reports and security reviews.

## Activities/method
Audit tool/provider name, approved use, prohibited use, access/data classification allowed, provider change risk, outage/continuity risk, security/privacy risk, cost/credit burn risk, support/reliability history, continued suitability and required monitoring.

## Outputs/records
Supplier/provider audit report, suitability decision and escalation actions.

## Audit criteria
Providers must remain suitable for controlled use and not expose customer or tenant data.

## Evidence required
Provider records, outage logs, approval evidence, monitoring evidence and risk review.

## MLA / maturity dependency
Higher automation maturity requires stronger supplier governance.

## Linked process
Supplier/tool governance and Clause 9 supplier audit.

## Linked gate
Supplier/provider approval gate.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when provider risk is controlled. HOLD when evidence is incomplete. BLOCKED when provider risk threatens security, continuity or customer-data protection.

## Escalation
Escalate outages, provider breaches, uncontrolled cost burn or unsupported use.

## Management-review input
Supplier risk and provider performance feed management review.

## Update trigger
Provider change, outage, audit finding, onboarding or security review.
