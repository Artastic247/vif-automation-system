# AUDIT_PROGRAMME

## Purpose
Plan and control risk-based and maturity-based audits for the Software Build Management System.

## Scope
All system, process, app, agent, skill, procedure, workflow, prompt, context, AI, security, evidence, release, validator, supplier, GitHub CI and n8n readiness audits.

## Owner/agent
Internal Audit Specialist with MLA Assessor and relevant specialist auditor.

## Inputs
Risk register, MLA level, prior findings, incidents, validation reports, management-review actions.

## Activities/method
Maintain: audit ID, audit type, item/process audited, maturity level, audit criteria, auditor/specialist, planned date, actual date, frequency, evidence required, result, findings, linked NC/RCA/CAPA reference, management-review input, status.

## Outputs/records
Approved audit programme, audit schedule, audit status and links to findings.

## Audit criteria
Audit frequency must be justified by risk, maturity and exposure. Standards are alignment lenses only; SOURCE REQUIRED or CUSTOMER SOURCE REQUIRED where applicable.

## Evidence required
Audit plan, sampled records, criteria, findings and closure evidence.

## MLA / maturity dependency
M0/M1 gap review; M2 pilot audit; M3 scheduled audit; M4 KPI/effectiveness audit; M5 risk-based automation-readiness audit.

## Linked process
Clause 9 performance evaluation.

## Linked gate
Audit programme gate.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when all critical scopes have assigned audits. HOLD when audit timing/evidence is incomplete. BLOCKED when high-risk or automation-related scope has no audit coverage.

## Escalation
Escalate overdue critical audits, major findings and unapproved automation expansion.

## Management-review input
Programme status, overdue audits, high-risk findings and maturity trends feed management review.

## Update trigger
Risk change, maturity change, incident, CI/n8n request, app onboarding request or management review.
