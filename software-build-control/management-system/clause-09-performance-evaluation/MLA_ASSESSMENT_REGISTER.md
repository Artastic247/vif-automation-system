# MLA_ASSESSMENT_REGISTER

## Purpose
Record maturity-level assessment results.

## Scope
All controlled items in the Software Build Management System.

## Owner/agent
MLA Assessor.

## Inputs
Item evidence, audit results, owner review, risk status.

## Activities/method
| Item ID | Item name | Item type | Owner/agent | Linked process | Linked skill/WI | Current maturity level | Evidence supporting maturity | Missing evidence | Risk | Interfaces affected | Control points | Audit frequency | Automation permission | Upgrade criteria | Downgrade trigger | Gate decision | Review date |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Outputs/records
Controlled maturity record.

## Linked process
Clause 9 performance evaluation.

## Linked gate
MLA gate.

## Evidence required
Artefact, records, audit evidence, improvement evidence.

## MLA / maturity dependency
Current level controls permission.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when evidence exists. HOLD when evidence is partial. BLOCKED when maturity is claimed without evidence.

## Escalation
Escalate BLOCKED maturity or automation conflict.

## Update trigger
Assessment, audit, incident, RCA/CAPA, release, or automation request.
