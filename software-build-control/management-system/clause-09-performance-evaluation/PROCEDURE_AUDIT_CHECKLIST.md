# PROCEDURE_AUDIT_CHECKLIST

## Purpose
Audit whether a procedure is operationally usable and not merely a document shell.

## Scope
All management-system and software-build procedures including design control, change control, app maintenance, audit, MLA, RCA/CAPA, release/rollback, CI governance and onboarding readiness.

## Owner/agent
Procedure Audit Specialist with Internal Audit Specialist.

## Inputs
Procedure file, linked process map, records, risks, interfaces, revision history, maturity assessment and use evidence.

## Activities/method
Check purpose/scope, process owner, inputs/outputs, controlled sequence, required records, risk controls, interfaces, PDCA loop, evidence requirements, escalation, revision control, maturity level and whether users can execute the procedure. Ask: what triggers it, who acts, what evidence proves completion, what gate does it control, and what stops unsafe work?

## Outputs/records
Procedure audit checklist, findings, required updates, NC/RCA/CAPA trigger where procedure failure contributed to nonconformity.

## Audit criteria
Procedure must define a usable sequence, roles, evidence, records, controls, interfaces and update triggers. Do not claim compliance/certification readiness.

## Evidence required
Procedure, records generated by procedure, revision history, linked job cards/gates, sample completed records.

## MLA / maturity dependency
M1 procedure may be draft only. M2 requires pilot use. M3 requires release approval. M4/M5 require KPI/effectiveness evidence.

## Linked process
Documented information, operational control, performance evaluation and continual improvement.

## Linked gate
Procedure release and audit gate.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when procedure is used and records prove it. HOLD when procedure exists but use evidence is weak. BLOCKED when procedure is missing, contradictory, unsafe, or bypasses required gates.

## Escalation
Escalate procedures enabling app-repo CI, n8n, release, schema/RLS or app changes without evidence.

## Management-review input
Major procedure gaps and overdue revisions feed management review.

## Update trigger
Audit finding, process change, RCA/CAPA, management review, standard/customer requirement change.
