# INTERNAL_AUDIT_PROCEDURE

## Purpose
Define internal audit method for the Software Build Management System.

## Scope
System, process, app/product, agent, skill/WI, procedure, workflow, prompt, context, AI, data/security, evidence, release, validator, supplier/tool, CI and n8n readiness audits.

## Owner/agent
Internal Audit Specialist.

## Inputs
Audit programme, criteria, scope, records, risk, maturity level and evidence.

## Activities/method
Plan audit, define scope/criteria/sample, collect objective evidence, classify findings, escalate, require action, feed RCA/CAPA and management review.

## Outputs/records
Audit plan, audit report, findings register, management-review input.

## Audit criteria
Approved procedures, registers, job cards, validation outputs, and standard lenses. SOURCE REQUIRED where official standard source is not supplied.

## Evidence required
Objective evidence, sampled records, finding evidence, closure evidence.

## MLA / maturity dependency
Audit frequency and depth depend on maturity and risk.

## Linked process
Clause 9 internal audit.

## Linked gate
Audit gate.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS if evidence satisfies criteria. HOLD if minor evidence gaps exist. BLOCKED if critical control missing, false claim found, or unsafe work is enabled.

## Escalation
Major finding, data risk, unsupported compliance claim, maturity overstatement.

## Management-review input
Audit findings and trends feed management review.

## Update trigger
Audit schedule, incident, management review, maturity change.
