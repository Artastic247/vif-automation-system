# AUDIT_CRITERIA_REGISTER

## Purpose
Define controlled audit criteria without unsupported compliance claims.

## Scope
All audit types and specialist audits.

## Owner/agent
Internal Audit Specialist and relevant standards specialist.

## Inputs
Procedures, WIs, gates, job cards, standards lenses, customer requirements and validation rules.

## Activities/method
Maintain: criteria ID, audit type, requirement/control checked, source basis, source status, linked process, linked WI, linked gate, expected evidence, PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED decision rule, responsible specialist.

## Outputs/records
Audit criteria register and source-status record.

## Audit criteria
Criteria must be approved, evidence-based and not copied from proprietary standards. Use SOURCE REQUIRED and CUSTOMER SOURCE REQUIRED where applicable.

## Evidence required
Source basis, interpretation owner, evidence expectation and approval status.

## MLA / maturity dependency
M3+ controlled use requires defined audit criteria.

## Linked process
Clause 9 internal audit.

## Linked gate
Audit criteria gate.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when criteria and source status are clear. HOLD when source evidence is missing but no customer claim is made. BLOCKED when unsupported criteria drive release, onboarding, automation or customer-facing claims.

## Escalation
Escalate unsupported compliance/certification claims to NC/RCA/CAPA.

## Management-review input
Criteria gaps and SOURCE REQUIRED items feed management review.

## Update trigger
New audit type, new standard lens, customer requirement or finding.
