# AUDIT_FINDINGS_REGISTER

## Purpose
Record, classify and track audit findings to closure.

## Scope
Findings from all Clause 9 audits and readiness reviews.

## Owner/agent
Internal Audit Specialist with finding owner.

## Inputs
Audit evidence, checklist result, risk assessment, maturity level and breached gate/control.

## Activities/method
Maintain: finding ID, audit ID, finding source, item affected, process affected, agent affected, skill affected, module affected, maturity level, requirement/gate breached, evidence, severity, containment required, RCA required, CAPA required, owner, due date and status.

## Outputs/records
Audit finding record, containment action, NC/RCA/CAPA trigger and closure evidence.

## Audit criteria
Findings must be objective, traceable to evidence and classified by risk and gate impact.

## Evidence required
Sampled evidence, breached control, risk/severity basis and closure evidence.

## MLA / maturity dependency
Major/repeat findings may downgrade maturity and block automation/onboarding.

## Linked process
Clause 9 audit and Clause 10 NC/RCA/CAPA.

## Linked gate
Finding closure gate.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS when finding is closed with evidence. HOLD when action is open. BLOCKED when critical risk is uncontrolled or containment is missing.

## Escalation
Escalate critical, overdue, repeat or false-PASS findings.

## Management-review input
Finding trends, repeats, overdue actions and critical risks feed management review.

## Update trigger
Audit finding, containment update, RCA/CAPA trigger or closure.
