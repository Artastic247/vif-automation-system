# N8N_READINESS_AUDIT_CHECKLIST

## Purpose
Assess whether n8n automation readiness remains controlled and correctly held before implementation.

## Scope
Notification workflows, issue creation, evidence collection, routing and future orchestration concepts.

## Owner/agent
n8n Automation Controller with MLA Assessor and Internal Audit Specialist.

## Inputs
Automation governance rules, maturity records, validator calibration records, authority matrix and onboarding readiness evidence.

## Activities/method
Audit whether process maturity is sufficient, MLA permits automation, authority matrix exists, validator calibration is sufficient, issue templates are controlled, evidence schema is controlled, privacy/data boundaries are controlled, rollback/disable route exists, audit route exists, RCA/CAPA route exists and human approval points are clear.

## Outputs/records
n8n readiness audit result, HOLD/BLOCKED decision and readiness gaps.

## Audit criteria
n8n remains HOLD until maturity, authority, validator and audit controls are proven.

## Evidence required
Maturity evidence, governance records, calibration records, approval matrix and rollback route.

## MLA / maturity dependency
n8n readiness depends on higher maturity and approved automation governance.

## Linked process
Automation governance and Clause 9 readiness audit.

## Linked gate
n8n readiness gate.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS not permitted in current phase. HOLD for notification-only, issue creation, evidence collection and auto-routing until separately approved. BLOCKED for auto-fix, auto-merge and auto-release.

## Escalation
Escalate any attempt to bypass maturity, authority or audit controls.

## Management-review input
n8n readiness risks and blocked conditions feed management review.

## Update trigger
Automation request, governance change, maturity change or audit finding.
