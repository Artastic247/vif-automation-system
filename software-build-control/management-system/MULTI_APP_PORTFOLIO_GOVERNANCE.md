# MULTI_APP_PORTFOLIO_GOVERNANCE.md

## Purpose
Define governance for managing multiple governed apps, shared operational controls, portfolio risk, cross-app dependencies and ecosystem-level maturity.

## Scope
Applies to all governed apps, shared governance assets, shared validators, shared prompts, shared operational controls, shared onboarding routes and future ecosystem-level automation.

## Owner/agent
System owner governs portfolio direction. VIF Orchestrator governs portfolio execution and routing. QA Gatekeeper governs ecosystem evidence and effectiveness review.

## Inputs
App inventory, onboarding status, maturity assessments, shared-component register, audit findings, KPI trends, CAPA status, release records, dependency risks and management-review decisions.

## Activities/checklist
1. Maintain governed app inventory.
2. Classify app maturity and operational status.
3. Review cross-app dependencies and shared assets.
4. Review ecosystem-level risks and bottlenecks.
5. Review tenant/data segregation across apps.
6. Review portfolio KPI trends.
7. Review release conflicts and dependency impacts.
8. Review validator and prompt reuse impacts.
9. Approve, hold or block portfolio-level rollout actions.
10. Feed portfolio lessons learned into process, skill, prompt and governance updates.

## Outputs/records
Portfolio review, dependency map, maturity review, ecosystem risk review, release coordination review and portfolio lessons learned.

## Maturity level
M3 required for governed multi-app operations. M4/M5 require managed ecosystem optimisation and predictive governance.

## Evidence required
App inventory, onboarding evidence, maturity ratings, dependency review, KPI evidence, release evidence, audit findings and CAPA status.

## Linked process
MOP-02 Leadership and governance, MOP-03 Strategic planning risk and opportunity, MOP-06 Management review, SOP-02 Organisational knowledge control, SOP-09 Configuration and version control.

## Linked agent/skill/procedure/module
System owner, VIF Orchestrator, QA Gatekeeper, Process Engineer, maturity assessment, portfolio review and shared-component governance.

## Interface/control point
Interfaces with onboarding governance, release governance, validator governance, prompt governance, AI governance, runtime monitoring, CAPA and management review.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
- PASS: portfolio risks, dependencies and governance remain controlled.
- HOLD: dependency, maturity or release conflicts require further review.
- BLOCKED: uncontrolled cross-app contamination, shared-component instability, tenant exposure or ecosystem-level release risk.

## PDCA
- PLAN: define portfolio objectives, dependencies and risks.
- DO: operate governed multi-app ecosystem.
- CHECK: review ecosystem evidence, KPIs and incidents.
- ACT: optimise governance, controls, skills and shared assets.

## Audit frequency
During management review, portfolio review cycles and after ecosystem-level incidents.

## Automation allowance
Portfolio-level automation remains blocked until ecosystem governance, dependency controls and operational maturity are proven.

## Escalation
Escalate ecosystem instability, dependency conflict, tenant/data exposure, shared-component drift, repeated release conflict or governance breakdown.

## Update trigger
New app onboarding, shared-component change, release conflict, ecosystem incident, audit finding, KPI trend issue or lesson learned.
