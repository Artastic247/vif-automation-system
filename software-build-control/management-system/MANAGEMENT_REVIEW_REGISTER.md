# MANAGEMENT_REVIEW_REGISTER

## Purpose
Review the suitability, adequacy, and effectiveness of the system.

## When to use
Scheduled reviews or major strategic changes.

## Owner/tool
Leadership/system owner.

## Inputs
KPIs, audits, incidents, risks, objectives, lessons learned, customer feedback.

## Activities or fields
| Review date | Inputs reviewed | Decisions | Actions | Owner | Due date | Effectiveness follow-up |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Outputs/records
Management review decisions and actions.

## Gate controlled
Management review gate.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
PASS if decisions/actions recorded. HOLD if inputs incomplete. BLOCKED if critical unresolved system risk is ignored.

## Update trigger
Review schedule or major change.
