# CAPA_REGISTER.md

## Purpose
Provide a controlled register for corrective and preventive actions.

## Scope
Applies to all CAPA actions raised from audits, NCs, AI/prompt failures, release issues, validator failures, maturity reviews and management-review actions.

## Owner/agent
RCA/CAPA Owner.

## Inputs
NC records, audit findings, RCA outputs, KPI trends, management-review actions and effectiveness-review results.

## Outputs/records
CAPA action records, implementation evidence, effectiveness review and closure records.

## Maturity level
Repeated ineffective CAPA blocks maturity increase.

## Evidence required
Root cause evidence, action evidence, effectiveness evidence and closure approval.

## Linked process
MOP-07 Corrective action and continual improvement.

## Linked agent/skill/procedure/module
RCA/CAPA Owner, QA Gatekeeper and effectiveness-review procedure.

## Interface/control point
Interfaces with audits, management review, maturity assessment and lessons learned.

## PASS/HOLD/BLOCKED rules
- PASS: CAPA effective and closed.
- HOLD: CAPA in progress.
- BLOCKED: ineffective CAPA or overdue critical CAPA.

## PDCA
- PLAN: define actions.
- DO: implement actions.
- CHECK: verify effectiveness.
- ACT: improve or escalate.

## Audit frequency
Reviewed during audits and management review.

## Automation allowance
Automation blocked where critical CAPA open.

## Escalation
Escalate overdue or ineffective CAPA.

## Update trigger
New RCA, audit finding, repeated failure or management-review action.
